  • I What we do

    Rotary & Rotaract

    Rotary International is a global community of commited professionals working together to serve others and advance peace. More than 1.2 million members in over 34000 Rotary clubs worldwide volunteer in communities at home and abroad. Rotaract is a service club for younf men and women ages 18 to 30 who are dedictaed to community and international service. Its membership totlas over 184,000 in more than 8000 clubs worlwide. Rotarct clubs are self-governing and self supporting and can be either University or community based. Individual Rotary clubs sponsor Rotaract Clubs and offer guidance and support, making the Rotaract clubs true "partners in service" and key members of the family of Rotary.

  • II Why we do what we do

    Here's to the Crazy Ones

    With the dawn of the new term, bigger responsibilities lie with us. It's time to bolster the foundation we rest upon, to take our endeavors to a more sustainable platform. With loyalty towards our vision and charity for all, we must embark on our phenomenal journey in making a society a better place. Be it far from this city of dust pockmarked with potholes, or in the heart of her thoroughfares, we shall bind up her wounds, and care for her people. And with the tonic of madness infused in us, we'll say : "Here's to the crazy ones. The misfits. The rebels. The troublemakers. The round pegs in the square holes. The ones who see differenly. They're not fond of rules. And they have no respect for the status quo. You can quote them, disagree with them, glorify or villify them. About the only thing you can't do is to ignore them. Because they change things. they push the human race forward. And while some may see them as the crazy ones, we see genius. Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones who do."
